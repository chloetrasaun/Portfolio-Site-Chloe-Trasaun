TUMZ

About The Project

TUMZ is a customizable tracking health resource for users with GI health disorders to track their symptoms and better understand their own individual condition. Offering Coaching Services, Tracking with pattern recognition, and access to a toolkit for flare ups.

Problem Statement

Gut health-interested people need a way to track their data for their gut health journey to fit their personal needs. There are not many application options for people with chronic gut health conditions to rely on to help them better their condition and better understand their body's triggers and symptoms, leading us to create TUMZ. We will know this to be true when we see that people can track and overview their data on their conditions and receive helpful personalized guides on how to cope while also having access to in-app recognition of any patterns regarding symptoms to possible triggers.

Solution

An app that provides useful resources and tracking methods for people with chronic gut health conditions to be able to track their data daily and have a better understanding of their gut health and what their triggers may be. It will also provide a reliable tool kit full of helpful coping methods that users will have access to any time they are experiencing a flare-up and informational pages they can read to help them better understand their condition.

My Process

Research

Within our Market Research process for TUMZ, we conducted a Competitive Analysis’ to get an idea of what other applications with similar purposes are out there. Within each of the competitor application’s strengths and weaknesses is valuable insight on what we can utilize to make TUMZ more effective and also what we can withhold from adding to avoid unnecessary features.

Interview Question

  • What kind of digestive disorder/s do you obtain?
  • What does your everyday life with (said disorder/s) look like?
  • How would you say your disorder sets you back or makes your life harder?
  • What kinds of practices or actions have you found that help reduce your symptoms or flare ups?
  • Have you ever tried tracking your symptoms for your disorder? Is so how did it go?
  • What are your feelings towards daily tracking of your symptoms and daily food intake/medications/stools on an app to better understand what your triggers are?

Ideation

After gathering all the needed information about the users and understanding their main needs, I created a user flow, with three defined keys: create an account, enter in tracking data, and how to utilize the toolkit.

Wireframes

Using the tool Procreate, I created the low-fidelity wireframes of TUMZ. Within this process, I mainly focused on the Mobile Coaching, Mobile Tracking, and Mobile Toolkit sections of the application. After designing an overall layout for each feature I moved on to creating Mid-Fidelity Wireframes using Figma.

As a result of User Testing the following elements were changed:

  • The Onboarding Process
  • The Simplicity of the Home Page
  • Personalization Features

Design

Home Screen

The home screen was designed in a way that everything that is most relevant to the user is easily accessible, with a clear interface.

Onboarding

Simplistic three screen slider that goes through each of the biggest features TUMZ has to offer.

Tracking Page

The most important feature of TUMZ allowing each of the users to track multiple personalized features anytime they need.

Toolkit Page

This page allows for the users to have 24/7 access to an emergency toolkit in place to help during hard mental days or flare ups.

Typography

I chose two fonts for the typography. Irish Grover for the titles and headers, which is bold and funky, and Intel for the body, which is the perfect combination for a simple and fun feel.

Color Palette

In order to keep the portfolio looking modern and simple, I chose six colors for the colour palette.
